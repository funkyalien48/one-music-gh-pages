BIO

Software Developer by day, musicial virtiuso by day - 22 year old Anya Solo is a self-taught songwriter, music producer, and multi-instrumentalist, which has exceeds societal expectations by thus-far completely individually creating her extensive musical portfolio. Among her many creative roles, she wears more hats - working in cinima arts and finding herself a student of life besides.

Previously known as Shib, Anya Solo makes art purely out of love. Out of her 15+ experience of writing, you will hear no one genre - rather, a cultivation of something entirely and earnestly personal.

In fact, only approximately 1% of this nerdy girls music is out in the world to be heard; the remaining 99% is stored safely on her Macbook - until further notice - once the perfectionism finds its way out of her consciousness. Or atleast, mutes itself ever so slightly..

Feel free to browse around, and contact her with any questions or business inquires. Always happy to make some art.

Thanks for listening and being here!
